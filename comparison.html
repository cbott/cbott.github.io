The Native American movement vs. the African American movement

The Native American movement differed in many ways from the African American struggle. The main difference was that Native Americans were often violent in their protests. They also had a much greater struggle in many aspects, as they were the poorest minority group , with 40% of Native Americans below the poverty line. Though the African American civil rights movement fought to gain full citizen's rights for blacks, Native Americans hoped to keep their tribes separate from the U.S. government.
